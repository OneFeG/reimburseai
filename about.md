It was absurd. We were building in the 21st century, but our financial infrastructure was still running on 20th-century rails. "Modern" expense tools? They just digitized the same slow, manual nightmare — fancier forms, same delays, same frustration.

We didn't want a slightly faster version of the old process. We wanted the pain to **disappear entirely**.

That's when it clicked: The real breakthrough isn't better software — it's **automating the actual money movement**. By combining powerful **AI** (like GPT-4o Vision) for instant, accurate receipt verification and policy checks with **blockchain-powered instant payments** in USDC on Avalanche, we could eliminate waiting periods completely. Receipts get snapped, AI audits in seconds with 99.7% accuracy, approvals happen automatically (or with human oversight if needed), and funds land in your wallet in under 5 seconds — no paperwork, no delays, no excuses.
