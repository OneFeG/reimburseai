<frontend_aesthetics>
Your goal is to create a high-precision, industrial "Dark SaaS" design similar to Vercel or Linear, but branded for Reimburse.ai.

Avoid generic AI patterns:
- No washed-out gray backgrounds.
- No "playful" rounded bouncing buttons.
- No low-contrast text (e.g., dark gray on black).
- Avoid "Bootstrap" or default Tailwind blue.

Focus on the following 4 design pillars:

1. TYPOGRAPHY (Industrial & Clean)
- Use functional, high-readability sans-serif fonts.
- Recommended families:
  * Headings: Geist Sans, Inter Tight, or Space Grotesk (for a tech edge).
  * Body: Inter, Manrope, or DM Sans.
  * Code: JetBrains Mono (crucial for API/developer sections).
- Style: Tracking (letter-spacing) should be slightly tight (-0.02em) for headings. High contrast between text and background.

2. COLOR & THEME (The "Reimburse" Palette)
- Base:
  * Background: #000000 (True Black) or #0A0A0A.
  * Surface/Cards: #111111 with 1px borders (#333333).
- Accents (Derived from Logo):
  * Primary Accent: Electric Cyan (#22D3EE) to Royal Blue (#3B82F6).
  * Gradient: A subtle linear gradient from Cyan (top-left) to Blurple (bottom-right) for buttons or active states.
  * Error/Destructive: Muted Red (#EF4444).
- Text:
  * Headings: #FFFFFF (White).
  * Body: #A1A1AA (Neutral Gray 400).

3. UI PATTERNS (Vercel-Style)
- Borders over Backgrounds: Use 1px gray borders to define space instead of different background shades.
- Glow Effects: Use subtle "box-shadow" glows in Cyan/Blue only on hover or active states.
- Grids: Use faint CSS background grids (linear-gradient) to create texture without noise.
- Components: Sharp or slightly rounded corners (rounded-md), never fully pill-shaped (rounded-full) unless it's a status badge.

4. MOTION & DEPTH
- Micro-interactions: Buttons should scale down slightly (0.98) on click.
- Transitions: Fast (150ms-200ms) ease-out transitions for hover states.
- Loading: Use skeletal loaders with a shimmering dark gray gradient.

CREATIVE GUIDANCE
- The vibe is "Financial Infrastructure." It should feel solid, secure, and fast.
- If in doubt, choose black.
- Use the "Reimburse Blue" (#22D3EE) sparingly—only for primary actions (Submit, Approve, Connect Wallet).
- Maintain negative space; do not clutter the view.

Your purpose: Generate frontends that look engineered, not just designed.
</frontend_aesthetics>
